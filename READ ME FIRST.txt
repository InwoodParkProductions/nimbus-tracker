========================================================
  NIMBUS TRACKER 3  —  by Inwood Park Productions
  Automatic camera tracking for Blender
========================================================

EVERYTHING IS INCLUDED. No installs, no downloads:
Blender, the AI models, everything ships inside this package.

HOW TO RUN
----------
1. EXTRACT THIS ZIP FIRST.
   Right-click the .zip  ->  "Extract All..."  ->  pick a folder.
   Do NOT run the app from inside the zip preview — extract the
   WHOLE folder first.

2. Open the extracted "Nimbus Tracker" folder and double-click:
        Nimbus Tracker.exe

3. First launch may show a blue Windows SmartScreen box
   ("Windows protected your PC") because the app isn't code-signed.
   Click  "More info"  ->  "Run anyway".

That's it. If your PC doesn't have Microsoft's WebView2 runtime,
the app opens in your web browser instead — either way it works.

REQUIREMENTS
------------
* Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit).
* An NVIDIA GPU makes the AI masking and rendering much faster,
  but everything falls back to CPU automatically without one.

WHAT IT DOES
------------
Open a video clip -> it splits it into camera shots -> AI masks the
people out (costumes, props and all) -> tracks the background ->
solves the camera -> drops the solved camera into your Blender
scene -> renders while you watch, live in Blender's own window.
When a shot's background is too plain for a full 3D solve, it falls
back to a best-effort motion match so your CG still follows the
camera.

Your settings live in:  %APPDATA%\NimbusTracker
Nothing is uploaded — all processing stays on your machine.
